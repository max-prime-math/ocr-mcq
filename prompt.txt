- The corresponding grading scheme / rubric is on the right side of the page.

The PDFs may also contain extra pages that should be ignored, such as:
- Title pages
- Cover sheets
- Instructions pages
- Section separators
- Other non-question pages

Your task is to design and implement a separate FRQ app with a workflow similar to the reference MCQ project, but tailored to FRQ extraction.
